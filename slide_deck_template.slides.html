Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the characteristics of gender, weekday and trip start time that could be used to predict members uses habits, The main focus was on gender, trip start time, weekday statistics and age groups.

Dataset Overview¶

The data consisted of 183412 row , 16 columns consist of time, numeric and catogrical columns, The attributes included time, trip duration, long/lat, gender, date of birth and customer type, Gender column, duration time column, week days column and age column are most interinsting for me.
